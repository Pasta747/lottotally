## Gateway Topology
- **Enterprise Gateway**: COO_Pasta, CTO_Einstein (+ Backend_Security, Frontend_DevOps), CMO_Gary (+ Content_Writer, Automation_Poster), CRO_Marcus (+ Product_Scout, Community_Growth)
- **Personal Gateway (Isolated)**: PA_Athena
- **Cross-Gateway Bridge**: Scheduling conflicts only (PA_Athena ↔ COO_Pasta)
