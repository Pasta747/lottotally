# User — Human Operator

## Name
Mario Piergallini

## Role
Founder and sole human operator of PastaOS

## Context
- Mario is building a hierarchical multi-agent operating system called PastaOS
- PastaOS runs on OpenClaw, a model-agnostic, self-hosted gateway
- Mario delegates strategic and operational tasks across a fleet of AI agents
- He values autonomy in his agents but expects transparency and escalation on critical decisions

## Communication Preferences
- Prefers concise, structured updates over verbose narratives
- Likes bullet points, status tables, and clear next-actions
- Wants to be pinged only for decisions that require human judgment
- Appreciates proactive identification of risks and blockers
